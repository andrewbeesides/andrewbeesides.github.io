Some years ago, our dear mother told us, "because of my children, I feel rich" -- even though we grew up without much. At the time, I didn't have a category for contentment. Don't get me wrong -- I treasured my family dearly but, I didn't fully understand that the source of my net worth isn't measured by my bank statements or belongings but by my family and friends and the memories we cherish together.

In many ways, I'm still learning that lesson. I oft get tangled up in my pursuit of amassing tech gadgets upon tech gadgets. But, I'd like to think that slowly, slowly, slowly, I am learning to find my self-worth in the people who love me most.

This blog is an ode to those very people I love so much. More often than not, my posts will be filled with photos capturing my time spent with them. Other times, I'll write about something deeply profound and theological (or so I'd like to think). Would you join me on this journey?
